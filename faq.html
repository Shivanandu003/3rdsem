Student Mental Health FAQs

What is student mental health?

Student mental health refers to the psychological well-being of students attending educational institutions. It includes emotional and psychological aspects of their overall health.

How does it affect students?

The mental health of students can affect various aspects of their lives, including academic performance, personal well-being, and overall quality of life.

How does it affect student's mental health?

Mood: Mental health issues, such as depression and anxiety, can lead to fluctuations in mood, affecting a student's ability to experience joy and satisfaction

How does it affect student'physical health?

Sleep Disturbances: Mental health problems often disrupt sleep patterns, leading to fatigue and physical health issues

Solutions

Orientation Programs: Colleges should provide orientation programs that include information about mental health resources and coping strategies Workshops and Seminars: Offer workshops on stress management, resilience, and mental health awareness.
